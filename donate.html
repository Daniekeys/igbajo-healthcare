Come and be part of the great vision and mission

Igbajo Baptist Church has recently started a new fundraising campaign for the construction of Igbajo Baptist Medical Center. As a Church whose mission is to help improve the health sector of Igbajo Community, we’re very excited to involve our community in our latest fundraising efforts.

With these funds, we will be able to make the construction of this health care facilities possible. To be part of how we plan to make a difference, Donations should be made to:
IGBAJO BAPTIST HOSPITAL , Zenith Bank 1214564605

In order to meet our fundraising goal of 100 million naira which is the estimated fee for the execution of this project., it will truly take a village. Are you able to support this incredible cause? Any amount you can contribute will make a difference.

Thank you for reading about our latest mission, and please reach out if you’d like to learn more and get involved with our fundraising efforts. As you give generously towards this project , which will benefit the community, it is our earnest prayer that God will in turn bless you , your family and business beyond measure.
